Welcome to the Warframe Reliquary

This is a prime wishlist, relic planning, and fissure tracking tool for Warframe

To get started, check a couple items on the Wishlist tab, then go to the Reliquary tab to see all the relevant information. Saving your wishlist is not necessary, but please read below for more information.

Wishlist tracking

You can check any item on the Wishlist page to mark it as wanted.

You can save your wishlist to gain extra functionality.

Vaulted items will be listed in red.

Relic planning

Relics containing any wishlisted items will be shown on the Reliquary page.

A list of starmap location groups currently know to reward these relics will be shown below the relic list. Hover or press on a group to see all the locations included.

Groups highlighted in gold currently have a fissure, and the fissure details will be listed at the bottom of the group.

Wishlist saving

While the wishlist can be used to plan your relic usage without being saved, saving it will generate two keys.

One private key, which you should bookmark for safekeeping. It will be labled "Bookmark".

One public key, which you can share with your friends. It will be labeled "Share"

Wishlist sharing

Once you and a friend have saved your wishlists, you can exchange public keys to include the data on eachother's reliquary pages.

Add any keys together with commas: wf.xuerian.net/af62z,nb90f

Relic system and rotations

The upper section of the Reliquary page will show you what relics contain what items.

The lower section of the page will show you what drop tables contain the relics themselves. This is not connected opening the relics, only obtaining them.

Rotations A, B, and C are rewarded to you like so:

  • A: 5/10 minutes, 5/10 waves, Excavator 1/2, Vault/Cache 1
  • B: 15 minutes, 15 waves, Excavator 3, Vault/Cache 2
  • C: 20 minutes, 20 waves, Excavator 4, Vault/Cache 3
